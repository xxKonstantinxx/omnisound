Privacy Policy

Omnisound · Last updated June 2026

Omnisound does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Omnisound has no accounts, no sign-in, and no servers. We do not collect names, emails, location, contacts, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or any other personal information.

Audio

All sound is generated and played entirely on your device. Omnisound does not record, upload, or share any audio. The app does not request microphone access.

Tracking & advertising

Omnisound contains no tracking, no third-party analytics, and no advertising. We do not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

Third parties

Omnisound does not include third-party SDKs that collect data, and it does not share information with anyone, because it collects none.

Children's privacy

Because Omnisound collects no personal data of any kind, it is safe for users of all ages, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
