# Omnisound — Press

**Omnisound turns iPhone and iPad into a one-finger chord harp — now an AUv3 plugin with MIDI.**

A loving homage to the electronic chord-harps of the 1980s: press one button to sound a full chord, sweep the strum plate to send it shimmering, add a rhythm, and record it straight into your music app.

## What it is

Omnisound is an electronic chord-and-strum instrument in the spirit of the beloved 1980s Omnichord — no keyboard skills, no music theory, no setup. Version 1.1 turns it into an **Audio Unit (AUv3) plugin** for GarageBand, AUM, Logic and other hosts, with full **MIDI in and out**, so you can record your playing straight into a session, play it from a MIDI keyboard, or drive other instruments.

## Highlights

- **36 chord buttons** — 12 roots across major, minor, and seventh rows, plus extended voicings; long-press to cycle variations
- **Strum plate** across four octaves, with a HOLD/TAP switch for sustained or momentary chords
- **Six chord voices** and **five string voices**, with two blendable harp layers
- **Ten rhythms** with auto-bass, plus a **chord-memory** sequencer
- Built-in melody keyboard, transpose, per-part tone and level controls
- **AUv3** with host presets and parameter automation, and **MIDI in/out**
