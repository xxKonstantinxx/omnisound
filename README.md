# Omnisound

**Strum-and-chord synth-harp for iPhone & iPad**

Omnisound is a playable electronic chord instrument inspired by the classic synth-harps of the 1980s. Tap a chord, sweep the sonic strings, add a rhythm — and you're making music in seconds.

## Quick start

- **Chords:** tap any of the 36 chord buttons. Long-press to cycle chord variations.
- **Sonic strings:** sweep your finger across the strum plate to arpeggiate the held chord.
- **Rhythm:** pick a pattern and set the tempo; turn on Bass Sync to follow your chords.
- **Melody:** play lead lines on the built-in keyboard.
- **Sound:** choose chord and string voices, and shape them with the tone knobs.

## FAQ

**Why is it landscape only?**
The full instrument — chords, strings, keyboard, and rhythm — is laid out side by side, which needs the width of landscape.

**Does it work with my other music apps?**
Yes. Omnisound mixes with other audio, and runs as an Audio Unit (AUv3) plugin inside hosts like GarageBand, AUM, and Logic, with MIDI in and out.

**Does it collect any data?**
No. Omnisound collects nothing and has no tracking. See the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).
